Average carbon dioxide (CO₂) levels in the atmosphere from 1959 to 2023

Atmospheric CO₂ levels hit a record 421.08 ppm in 2023. Pre-industrial levels averaged 280 ppm, but human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation have driven CO₂ over 50% higher.
